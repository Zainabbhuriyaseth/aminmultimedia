Top 5 Photography Tips for Beginners

Whether you're using a DSLR or a mirrorless camera, mastering the fundamentals of photography helps you take better shots in any situation. Here are five essential tips to get you started:

  1. Understand Lighting: Light is everything in photography. Practice shooting during golden hour and avoid harsh midday sun.
  2. Use the Rule of Thirds: Divide your frame into 9 squares and place subjects at intersections for better composition.
  3. Keep Your Lens Clean: A dirty lens ruins sharpness and clarity. Use a microfiber cloth before each shoot.
  4. Practice Manual Mode: Learn how aperture, shutter speed, and ISO work together to control exposure.
  5. Take More Photos: The best way to improve is by experimenting and learning from your mistakes.
